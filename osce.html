▶ Case 1: Chest Pain (ER)

Scenario: 55-year-old male with sudden chest pain

Your Task:

  • Take focused history
  • Assess risk factors
  • Give diagnosis + plan

Key Questions:

  • Onset, character, radiation?
  • Associated symptoms (sweating, SOB)?
  • Risk factors (HTN, smoking)?

Likely Diagnosis: Acute Coronary Syndrome


▶ Case 2: Shortness of Breath

Scenario: 30-year-old with difficulty breathing

Your Task:

  • Assess airway and breathing
  • Perform respiratory exam
  • Give differential diagnosis

Differentials:

  • Asthma
  • Pneumonia
  • Pulmonary embolism

▶ Case 3: Severe Anemia

Scenario: Patient with fatigue and Hb 6.5 g/dL

Your Task:

  • Identify severity
  • Look for cause
  • Plan management

Management:

  • Blood transfusion
  • Iron studies
  • Treat underlying cause

  4. Give impression and plan

⚪ OSCE Guide

ABCDE Approach

  • A: Airway – Check if patient can speak
  • B: Breathing – RR, SpO₂, chest movement
  • C: Circulation – Pulse, BP, cap refill
  • D: Disability – GCS, pupils, glucose
  • E: Exposure – Full body exam

SBAR Communication

  • S – Situation
  • B – Background
  • A – Assessment
  • R – Recommendation
