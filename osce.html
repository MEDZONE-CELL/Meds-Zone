ABCDE Approach

  • A – Airway: Ensure airway is patent
  • B – Breathing: RR, O2 saturation
  • C – Circulation: Pulse, BP
  • D – Disability: AVPU / GCS
  • E – Exposure: Full exam

SBAR

  • S – Situation
  • B – Background
  • A – Assessment
  • R – Recommendation

System Examination

  • General inspection
  • Vitals
  • Cardiovascular exam
  • Respiratory exam
  • Abdominal exam
  • Neurological exam
