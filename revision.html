⚡ Quick Revision (By System)

🫁 Respiratory

Pneumonia

  • Fever, cough, chest pain
  • Crackles, dull percussion
  • CXR confirms
  • Antibiotics

Pulmonary TB

  • Chronic cough, weight loss
  • Night sweats
  • GeneXpert
  • Start anti-TB

Asthma

  • Wheeze, SOB
  • Reversible airway obstruction
  • Give bronchodilators
❤️ Cardiovascular

Heart Failure

  • SOB, leg swelling
  • Raised JVP
  • Diuretics

Myocardial Infarction

  • Chest pain → arm
  • Sweating
  • ECG changes
  • Aspirin + oxygen
🍽 Gastrointestinal

Acute Abdomen

  • Severe abdominal pain
  • Guarding
  • Surgical emergency

Peptic Ulcer Disease

  • Epigastric pain
  • Relieved by food
  • PPI treatment
🧠 Neurology

Stroke

  • Sudden weakness
  • Facial droop
  • CT scan

Meningitis

  • Fever, neck stiffness
  • Altered consciousness
  • Start antibiotics urgently
🩸 Hematology

Severe Anemia

  • Pallor, fatigue
  • Hb ↓
  • Blood transfusion if severe
🧪 Endocrine

Hypoglycemia

  • Confusion, sweating
  • Low glucose
  • Give glucose
🚨 Emergency

Shock

  • BP ↓, Pulse ↑
  • Poor perfusion
  • IV fluids

Sepsis

  • Infection + organ dysfunction
  • Fever
  • Antibiotics urgently
